A little about myself

While my "stage name" is Zakki Blakk, my real name is Zak Knecht, and I'm an established videographer and rock n' roll-loving dude. I've got an un-scratchable itch to stay as creative as humanly possible during my time on this spinning chunk of space dirt we call Earth. I love all things camera and tech-related, in addition to a passion for guitars, music, video games, pets, '80s fashion, and the outdoors. I take my work seriously, but fully believe life is too short to not be as goofy and fun as possible. And I'm always searching for bigger and crazier adventures on the horizon!!

I started this video journey way back during my middle school years in which my brother and me showcased our hilarious / embarassing "snow stunts" in backyard home videos. Always chasing that adolescent joy of two brothers' goofiness, I've been hooked on the limitless narrative possibilities that video affords to this day. From shooting the smallest projects, to editing TV shows, to working with touring-level bands, to working with world-renowned yoga teachers... and I enjoy every aspect of the creative process!

I am also a self-taught musician currently working on my debut album. I live, eat, and breathe metal music and am always down to collaborate or jam! To see more about what I'm working on, click here!

Thanks for stopping by!!
