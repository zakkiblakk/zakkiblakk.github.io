Who Am I?

My real name is Zak Knecht, and I'm a 20-some year old videographer and rocker dude. I've got an unrelenting urge to stay as creative as humanly possible during my time here on this spinning chunk of space debris we call home. I love all things camera and tech-related, in addition to a passion for guitars, music, pets, '80s fashion, and the outdoors. I'm a super easy-going guy and am always looking to try new creative outlets and mediums!

I've started this video journey way back in my middle school years. My brother and I showcased our hilarious-yet-embarassing "snow stunts" in backyard home videos. Ever since I've been hooked on the limitless narrative possibilities that video affords us creators. From shooting the smallest projects, to working with touring-level bands, to working with world-class yoga teachers... I've enjoyed every aspect of the creative process. As a detail-oriented person, video projects both satisfy and fuel my creative urges right down to every last minute detail, lighting setup, or editing choice. And I'm always looking to go bigger!

I am also a self-taught musician currently working on my debut album. I play (and enjoy all genres of) metal and am always down to collaborate or jam! To see more about what I'm working on, click here!

Thanks for stopping by!! //Zakki
